# Settings

The Settings area lives under the **Ink Player** admin menu → **Settings**. A left sidebar lets you move between each settings section. Whenever you change a value, a Save bar appears at the bottom of the page with **Save** and **Discard** buttons — your changes aren't applied until you save.

## Sections

- **[Branding](/settings/branding)** — Add your logo and brand color so every player matches your site.
- **[Analytics](/settings/analytics)** — Turn on playback tracking and control how long data is kept.
- **[Presets](/settings/presets)** — Choose the default video and audio presets that new media start with.
- **[Sharing](/settings/sharing)** — Choose where Share links and embeds open on your site.
- **[Ads](/settings/ads)** — Run linear video ads, and enable or disable every ad site-wide.
- **[Advanced](/settings/advanced)** — Inject custom CSS/JS and decide what happens to your data on uninstall.
- **[Webhooks](/settings/webhooks)** — Send Ink Player payloads (such as captured leads) to any URL you choose.
- **[Integrations](/settings/integrations)** — Connect external services and media sources.

::: tip
Most settings apply globally. Where it matters, a specific [preset](/settings/presets) can override the global defaults for individual media.
:::
