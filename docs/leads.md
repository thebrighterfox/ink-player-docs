# Leads

**Leads** (`/leads`) collects email addresses from people watching your players, so you can grow your list and feed contacts into your email or CRM tools.

## How leads are captured

An **email-gate overlay** can require viewers to enter their email address to keep watching. When a viewer submits their email, it becomes a lead.

## The leads table

Captured leads appear in the table with:

- **Email**
- **Destination provider** — where the lead is delivered
- **Related content** — the media the lead came from
- **IP address**
- **Source**
- **Date**

You can also **add** and **edit** leads manually.

### Filters

Find leads quickly with the search box, a **date-range** filter, and **provider** and **type** filters.

## Delivering leads

Leads can be sent to your connected services automatically. You can deliver to email/CRM integrations, to Webhooks, or both:

- **Mailchimp**
- **MailerLite**
- **ActiveCampaign**
- **FluentCRM**
- **Webhooks** — see [Settings → Webhooks](/settings/webhooks)

::: tip Connect a destination first
Configure your email/CRM connections under [Integrations](/settings/integrations) before turning on lead delivery, so captured emails flow straight into your list.
:::
