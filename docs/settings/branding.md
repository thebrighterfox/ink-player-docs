# Branding

Branding lets you stamp every player with your own logo and accent color so the player feels like part of your site. Branding is **on by default**.

## Enable or disable branding

A master toggle sits in the **top-right of the section's card header**. Switch it off to remove all branding from your players; switch it on to apply the settings below.

## Branding options

When branding is enabled, you can configure:

### Logo

1. Click to open the WordPress media library.
2. Pick or upload an image.

The logo is shown as a watermark on the player chrome where supported.

::: tip
PNG or SVG files are recommended — they keep crisp edges and support transparency.
:::

### Logo max width

Set the maximum width of the logo, in pixels. This caps how large the watermark appears regardless of the source image size.

### Brand color

Choose the accent color used across the player — for buttons, progress bars, and theme highlights.

## How branding applies

Branding applies **globally to every player**. The only exception is when a specific [preset](/settings/presets) defines its own branding overrides — in that case, the preset wins for the media that uses it.

::: info
Remember to **Save** from the Save bar at the bottom of the page after making changes.
:::
