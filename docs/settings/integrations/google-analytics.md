# Google Analytics

Google Analytics is Google's web analytics service. When this integration is enabled and configured, Ink Player forwards player events — such as plays — to your Google Analytics property, so playback shows up alongside the rest of your site analytics.

## What this integration does

- Sends player events (like plays) to your Google Analytics property.
- Lets you see playback activity within your existing Google Analytics reports.

## How to connect

1. Go to **Settings → [Integrations](/settings/integrations)**.
2. Find the **Google Analytics** card under the **Analytics** category.
3. Click **Enable**, then **Manage**.
4. Connect Ink Player to your Google Analytics **property**.
5. Click **Save**.

::: info
This integration is separate from Ink Player's own built-in [Analytics](/analytics), which tracks playback without any external service. Google Analytics is for surfacing player events inside your existing Google reporting.
:::

## Requirements

- A Google Analytics account with a property to send events to.

## Notes

- Events may take some time to appear in Google Analytics reports.
- For playback insights that work out of the box — no external service required — use Ink Player's built-in [Analytics](/analytics).
