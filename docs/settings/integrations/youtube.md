# YouTube

YouTube is a **Video** integration. With it enabled, you can add a YouTube video to your library simply by pasting its URL — Ink Player fetches the details and plays it through YouTube's embed.

## What the integration does

The YouTube integration enables **metadata fetching**. When you paste a watch URL, Ink Player automatically pulls in the:

- **Title**
- **Poster** image
- **Duration**

## Connecting YouTube

1. Go to **Settings → Integrations** and enable **YouTube** (Video category).
2. That's it — no credentials are required.

## Adding a YouTube video

1. Start adding new media.
2. Paste a YouTube **watch URL**, in either form:
   - `https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=…`
   - `https://youtu.be/…`
3. Ink Player auto-fetches the title, poster, and duration.
4. Save the media item.

The video plays through **YouTube's embed**.

::: warning
Platform embeds **can't be truly access-protected**. You can place a YouTube video on a gated page, but the underlying video remains reachable on YouTube itself. See [Access control](/guide/access-control) for what protection is and isn't possible with embedded platforms.
:::

::: info
Added videos appear in your [Media](/media) library alongside your other content.
:::
