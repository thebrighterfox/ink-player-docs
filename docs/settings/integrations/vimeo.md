# Vimeo

Vimeo is a **Video** integration. With it enabled, you can add a Vimeo video to your library by pasting its URL — Ink Player fetches the details and plays it through Vimeo's embed.

## What the integration does

When you paste a Vimeo URL, Ink Player automatically fetches the video's **metadata** so you don't have to enter it by hand.

## Connecting Vimeo

1. Go to **Settings → Integrations** and enable **Vimeo** (Video category).
2. That's it — no credentials are required.

## Adding a Vimeo video

1. Start adding new media.
2. Paste a **Vimeo URL**.
3. Ink Player auto-fetches the video's metadata.
4. Save the media item.

The video plays through **Vimeo's embed**.

::: warning
Platform embeds **can't be truly access-protected**. You can place a Vimeo video on a gated page, but the underlying video remains reachable on Vimeo itself. See [Access control](/guide/access-control) for what protection is and isn't possible with embedded platforms.
:::

::: info
Added videos appear in your [Media](/media) library alongside your other content.
:::
